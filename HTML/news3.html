To fill 6,491 empty seats group 4 exam is conducted today in which 17 lakh people's are appearing for the exam.

To fill the 6,491 empty seats for the posting of VAO and other related post in Tamil Nadu government TNPSC group 4 exam is conducted today. An approximate of 17 lakh people is appearing for he exam. So an exam will be conducted to fill these postion was announced by the civil service exam department in june 14. Last date for online application was on july 14. Hall ticket was issued on august 22. Exam is conducted today all over the state from 10am to 1pm. A total of 3,000 centers were allocated for the exam. Strict rules are applied for the one's who is taking part in the exam. Reporting time for the exam is 9.30am. Only pen and hall ticket is allowed inside the examination hall no other electronic devices is allowed. No mall practice is allowed if found they will not be allowed to take the exam further. All arrangements are ready for the exam and an approximate of 1 lakh people is alloted for this arrangement purpose. There is lot of competation for VAO posting.
